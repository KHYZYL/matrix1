About Our Event

MATRIX is an Intercollegiate Management Festival

that nurtures future business leaders by blending theoretical insights with practical problem-solving. Through activities like case competitions, marketing challenges, and management quizzes, students can showcase their skills and enhance their strategic thinking.

MATRIX highlights cultural events that honor creativity in music, dance, and the arts, creating a vibrant atmosphere. Ultimately, MATRIX serves as a hub where innovative ideas thrive, effective strategies are developed, and students evolve into the business leaders of tomorrow.
